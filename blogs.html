Emergency Contact QR Code kya hai?

Emergency Contact QR ek simple QR code hota hai jisme aap apne urgent contact details store kar sakte hain, jo emergency mein jaldi access kiya ja sakta hai.

Kaise use karein?

Aap QR code generate kar sakte hain, usse print kar ke apne wallet, phone case ya kisi bhi important jagah chipka sakte hain. Emergency situation mein, koi bhi scanner se ye details dekh sakta hai.
